Burlington open device lab

Visit the lab

The Burlington Open Device Lab provides access to mobile devices for software testing to the Burlington, Vermont-area tech community.

About the project

The lab aims to help the local tech community develop and test their software across a wide range of devices, and was started by Union Street Media

Visit the lab

Feel free to stop in and use the devices. We're just a short walk from downtown, and just up the hill from the South End Arts District. View location

Help the lab grow

Anyone can participate and help the Burlington tech community by lending or donating to the open device lab. Participate

Visit the Lab

The lab space is kindly provided by Union Street Media, and is located at 444 South Union Street in Burlington's south end. Before visiting, please make sure that the space is available by contacting @btvodl. The lab is typically open during normal business hours.
