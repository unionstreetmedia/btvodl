Burlington open device lab

Visit the lab

The Burlington Open Device Lab provides access to mobile devices for software testing to the Burlington, Vermont-area tech community.

About the project

Union Street Media created and launched the Burlington Open Device Lab to help the local tech community, including local startups, small businesses, students, and hobbyists.
